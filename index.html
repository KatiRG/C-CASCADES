CO2 fluxes across countries and regions from freshwater sources
CO2 flux estimates over the period 2003–2012 from the LOAC budget showing contributions (mean [min, max] in Tg CH4 yr-1) from 3 freshwater source categories (Rivers, Lakes, and Estuaries) in terms of 3 latitude bands (High Latitudes, Mid Latitudes, and the Tropics) and 6 continental regions (Africa, Asia, Europe, North America, South America, and Oceania).
